─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PURPOSE OF THIS DOCUMENT

This document explains how Shane Bruce Mental Health & Coaching collects,
stores, protects, and uses your personal and clinical information. By signing
or acknowledging this consent, you confirm that you have read, understood, and
agreed to these practices.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. WHO WE ARE

Shane Bruce Mental Health & Coaching is a licensed therapy and coaching
practice based in Santa Barbara, California. We provide individual therapy,
coaching, and telehealth services. This practice is supervised by a licensed
Marriage and Family Therapist (MFT).

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

2. WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect two categories of information:

  A. Personal Information (PII)
     This includes your name, email address, phone number, date of birth,
     home address, emergency contact, insurance details, and payment
     preferences. This information is used to manage your care, communicate
     with you, and process billing.

  B. Clinical Information
     This includes the reason you are seeking therapy, your mental health
     history, medications, session notes written by your therapist, your
     responses to clinical assessments, and any journal entries or messages
     you submit through the client portal.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

3. HOW YOUR DATA IS STORED

Your information is stored on a HIPAA-compliant platform hosted by Google
Firebase (Google Cloud Infrastructure), which maintains industry-standard
security certifications including SOC 2 and ISO 27001.

We use a two-layer data model designed to protect your privacy:

  Layer 1 — Personal Information
  Your name, contact details, insurance, and other identifying information
  are stored in a secure, access-controlled database. This layer is never
  shared with or accessed by AI systems.

  Layer 2 — Clinical Information
  Your clinical data (session notes, assessments, journal entries, chat
  history) is stored separately from your personal information, in a
  de-identified format. This means your name and contact details are not
  attached to your clinical records in this layer.

These two layers are linked by a unique identifier known only to your
therapist and the system. This separation is a deliberate safeguard to
protect your identity in the event of any unauthorized access.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

4. WHO HAS ACCESS TO YOUR DATA

  - Your assigned therapist has access to your full clinical record,
    including session notes, assessments, and journal entries.

  - Administrative staff (billing and office roles) may access your personal
    and insurance information for scheduling and billing purposes only.
    They do not have access to your clinical notes or AI-generated content.

  - No one outside of Shane Bruce Mental Health & Coaching will access your
    data without your written consent, except as required by law (see
    Section 7).

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

5. HOW WE USE ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE (AI)

Our platform uses AI tools to support — not replace — your therapist. Here
is exactly how AI is used and, equally important, how it is not used:

  WHAT AI DOES:
  - Assists your therapist in reviewing clinical notes and suggesting
    treatment plan components for their consideration.
  - Powers a between-session support chat available through your client
    portal, where you can reflect, journal, and receive general guidance.
  - Analyzes de-identified clinical assessment responses to help your
    therapist identify patterns and areas of focus.

  WHAT AI DOES NOT DO:
  - AI never has access to your name, email, phone number, address, or any
    other personally identifying information.
  - AI never makes clinical decisions independently. Every AI-generated
    suggestion is reviewed and approved by your licensed therapist before
    it influences your care.
  - AI is never used to replace a human therapy session without your
    explicit agreement.

  AI SUPERVISION:
  All AI activity within the platform is monitored and controlled by a
  licensed therapist. Your therapist sets the goals, context, and boundaries
  for any AI interactions related to your care.

  THIRD-PARTY AI PROVIDERS:
  AI features may be powered by third-party services such as Anthropic
  (Claude) or OpenAI (ChatGPT). These providers process only de-identified
  clinical data — your personal information is never transmitted to them.
  We have or will maintain a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with any
  AI provider used in your care, as required under HIPAA.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

6. HOW LONG WE KEEP YOUR DATA

We retain your records in accordance with California law, which requires
mental health records to be kept for a minimum of 7 years from the date of
last service, or 7 years after a minor client turns 18, whichever is later.

After the retention period, records are securely and permanently deleted.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

7. WHEN WE MAY DISCLOSE YOUR INFORMATION WITHOUT CONSENT

Federal and California law require or permit disclosure of your information
in limited circumstances, including:

  - Immediate risk of harm to yourself or others.
  - Suspected abuse or neglect of a child, elder, or dependent adult.
  - A court order or valid legal subpoena.
  - Coordination of care with other treating providers (with your consent).

We will always notify you of any disclosure when legally permitted to do so.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

8. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  - Access your personal and clinical records upon written request.
  - Request corrections to information you believe is inaccurate.
  - Request that your data be deleted, subject to legal retention
    requirements.
  - Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time. Withdrawal will
    not affect the lawfulness of processing that occurred before withdrawal.
  - Receive a copy of this notice at any time by contacting us.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

9. DATA SECURITY

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  - All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).
  - Access to client data requires authenticated login with email
    verification.
  - Role-based access controls ensure that each staff member can only see
    the information they need to do their job.
  - Clinical data is structurally separated from personal data at the
    database level.
  - Our platform undergoes regular security reviews.
