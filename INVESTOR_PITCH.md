# Mindbridge — Investor Pitch Overview
### AI-Augmented Therapy Platform | Shane Bruce Mental Health & Coaching

## What We Are Building

Mindbridge is a HIPAA-compliant, AI-augmented therapy and mental health platform built for multi-associate practices. It combines a structured clinical data system with AI tools that help therapists work smarter, serve more clients, and deliver a deeper, more continuous experience — without sacrificing safety or clinical quality.

The platform serves two sides simultaneously:

- **Therapists and associates** get a dashboard that organizes client data, assists with diagnosis, generates treatment plan drafts, surfaces trends across sessions, and reduces administrative overhead.
- **Clients** get a private portal where they can access their progress notes, write in a personal journal visible to their therapist, and interact with an AI assistant that provides support between sessions — all within a clinically guided framework.

Every AI interaction is supervised and controlled by a licensed therapist in the background. The AI does not operate independently. It is a tool that multiplies the reach and depth of real clinical care.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Mental health care has a scalability problem. A single licensed therapist can realistically see 25–30 clients per week in a traditional model — each requiring a dedicated 50-minute session. That ceiling limits revenue, limits access, and keeps the cost of care high.

At the same time, millions of people need mental health support but can't afford weekly sessions, can't get an appointment, or need something between sessions that currently doesn't exist.

We bridge that gap.

## Therapist Capacity Model

The core question for scalability is: **how many clients can one therapist responsibly manage per month?**

A full-time therapist or associate works approximately **160 hours per month**, with roughly 30 hours dedicated to administration, documentation, and platform management — leaving about **130 hours of available client time**.

### Time Investment Per Client Per Tier

| Tier | Therapist Time Per Client / Month |
|------|----------------------------------|
| Tier 1 — AI Supervised | ~30 min (review AI notes, safety scan, goal adjustment) |
| Tier 2 — Hybrid | ~2.5 hrs (2 sessions × 50 min + review + notes) |
| Tier 3 — Full Care | ~4.5 hrs (4 sessions × 50 min + extended review + notes) |

### Estimated Client Capacity Per Associate (Mixed Portfolio)

| Tier | Clients | Hours Used |
|------|---------|------------|
| Tier 1 | 50 clients × 0.5 hr | 25 hrs |
| Tier 2 | 15 clients × 2.5 hrs | 37.5 hrs |
| Tier 3 | 8 clients × 4.5 hrs | 36 hrs |
| **Total** | **73 clients** | **~99 hrs** (within the 130 hr range, with buffer) |

**Conservative working estimate: ~70 clients per associate per month.**

This is roughly 2–3× the capacity of a traditional therapy practice, enabled by the AI layer handling routine check-ins, journaling, and between-session support.

## Why This Scales

**1. Each new associate multiplies capacity.**
Adding one trained associate adds ~70 client slots and ~$8,400/month in revenue. The marginal cost of the platform per additional user is low.

**2. The AI layer becomes more effective over time.**
As more structured, HIPAA-compliant clinical data accumulates, the AI tools improve — better treatment plan suggestions, more accurate session summaries, better between-session support. This creates a data moat that is hard for competitors to replicate.

**3. The supervised AI model is defensible and safe.**
Fully automated mental health apps face serious regulatory and safety risks. Our model keeps a licensed therapist in the loop at every tier, giving us a credible, compliant, and ethically sound path forward — even as regulations tighten.

**4. Demand is enormous and underserved.**
The global mental health market is valued at over $500 billion. Waitlists for therapy are months long in many areas. Subscription-based access to ongoing, AI-augmented care addresses a real and urgent gap.
